# Quickstart: Wealth Extraction

## Overview

The "Wealth Extraction" feature allows users to model money flowing *from* their accumulated wealth (Savings, Investments) back *into* their daily cash flow. This is useful for retirement planning (pensions) or large asset purchases (down payments).

## How to Use

1.  **Fixed Costs (Recurring)**:
    - Go to **Fixkosten**.
    - Click **Add Cost** (or edit existing).
    - Select **Entnahme** (Wealth Extraction) in the type toggle (Blue icon).
    - Enter a positive amount.
    - Save.
    - **Result**: Increases Monthly Surplus, Decreases Wealth Forecast.

2.  **Special Costs (One-time)**:
    - Go to **Sonderkosten**.
    - Add a new cost.
    - Select **Entnahme**.
    - **Result**: Increases liquidity for that month, Decreases Wealth Forecast.

## Visual Indicators

- **Blue**: Wealth Extraction (Entnahme)
- **Amber**: Saving (Sparen)
- **Green**: Income (Einnahme)
- **Red**: Expense (Ausgabe)
